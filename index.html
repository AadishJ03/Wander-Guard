While travelling to tourist places,especially in mountains and other trekking places,family members(oftenly kids) gets seperated from each other. Wander Guard is here to solve this problem,so that you never loose your loved ones as losing your loved ones might be the worst thing that can happen to anyone.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

  • A one in a kind of unique smart ID card that can track its wearers location at any remote location even in mountain peaks.
  • The ID Card will comprise of tag number that can help uniquely identify the location of its wearer.
  • It is equipped with a GPS Tracker and a adruino chip that sends the location of the user continuously to the user.
  • With this,the Committee as well as the wearers family will be able to track the required person.
  • There will be a minimal security deposit to account for the incurring costs.
